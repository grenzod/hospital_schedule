MEDCARE GROUP

Comprehensive Healthcare Services

History & Development Timeline

2001

Founded as MEDCARE General Clinic with an initial team of 5 staff members.

2007

Expanded into MEDCARE Diagnostic and Treatment Center in Ba Dinh, Hanoi.

2015

Officially became MEDCARE General Hospital at Nguyen Van Cu, Long Bien, Hanoi.

2018

Launched a network of satellite clinics across inner districts of Hanoi.

2020

Established the Digital Health Technology Center for telemedicine and online health consultations.

2025

By April 2025: 15 clinics nationwide, 26 branches, 120 mobile lab units, and offices in Singapore & Cambodia.

Mission & Vision

Mission

"MEDCARE is committed to delivering high-quality, compassionate, and timely healthcare services for the community's well-being."

Vision by 2030

  • To become the leading private healthcare group in Southeast Asia.
  • To offer integrated services of clinics, diagnostics, pharmaceuticals, and medical equipment.
  • To expand home healthcare and telemedicine services to 90% of provinces.
